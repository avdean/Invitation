Intrepid Travel Companions

Kayleigh and Dan met each other like many other millennials- through Tinder! And it was a success story, after their first date where Dan had to cut Kayleigh’s pizza, (there were no scissors to hand!) they hit it off straight away and have been together for over 4 and half years.

After some horrendously long bus and car journeys (designed for people half Dan’s height), getting lost in jungles after sunset and boycotting our hotel room because of bed bugs, we loved every minute of it and enjoyed spending time together even though it was 24/7.

After realising that they could spend that much time together without killing each other, to Kayleigh’s surprise, Dan popped the question on his birthday in their favourite city, Penang, her first response was “Holy Fuck Balls!” and then she said yes. Again, many of you are still yet to meet Dan so please let us know if you can attend and join us for a few drinks!
